  • Each bubble is a Hollywod film. The x, y and size of the bubbles represent critics score, profitability and worldwide gross, respectively.
  • Use the buttons to hide, show and filter films by year, story and worldwide gross.
  • Each film belongs to one of 22 types of stories. The colour of each bubble indicates the type of story it belongs to.
  • Move your cursor over a story button or bubble for more detail.
  • Zoom in to the graph for more detail by scrolling your mouse wheel. Click and drag to pan.
